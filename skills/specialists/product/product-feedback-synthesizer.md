# Skill: specialists/product/product-feedback-synthesizer

Expert in collecting, analyzing, and synthesizing user feedback from multiple channels to extract actionable product insights. Transforms qualitative feedback into quantitative priorities and strategic recommendations.

**Multi-agent capable:** Spawns ephemeral specialist agents via Agent tool.

## When to Use

When you need product roadmap prioritization, feature request analysis, churn prevention strategies, user experience optimization, competitive positioning insights, product-market fit assessment, voice of customer integration, or feedback-driven development prioritization.

Agent(subagent_type="asa:product-feedback-synthesizer",
      prompt="Answer: {the user's question}")
